Darjeeling



Facts about Darjeeling :
  1. Darjeeling is a town in northeast India that is famous for tea, trains and scenic beauty.
  2. Darjeeling tea is thin, light colored, aromatic and soothing to drink.
  3. The leaves are grown in the hills around the town.
  4. Access is by a three-hour car ride from the nearest airport or a seven-hour journey on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railroad, a narrow gauge railway known as the toy train.
