Andaman Islands



Facts about Hyderabad's Havelock Island:
  1. Ecotourism is encouraged at Havelock Island, the largest island in Ritchie Archipelago in the Andaman Islands.
  2. Though not as crowded as other islands in Asia, the number of visitors to Havelock Island is on the rise due to its great beaches, casual atmosphere, snorkeling and scuba diving opportunities.
  3. The best time to visit is mid-January to mid-May, when the weather is sunny with calmer seas.
  4. Redhanagar Beach is considered one of the best beaches in Asia.
